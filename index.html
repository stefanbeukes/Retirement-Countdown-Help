Retirement Countdown - Support

About the App

Retirement Countdown helps you track the time remaining until your retirement, including working days, holidays, and vacation time.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set my retirement date?

Open the app, tap on Settings, and use the date picker to select your retirement date.

How are working days calculated?

Working days are calculated based on your selected working days of the week, minus public holidays and your annual vacation days.

How do I customize the widget?

In the app settings, you can customize the widget appearance including fonts, colors, and what information is displayed.

Why aren't my country's holidays showing?

Make sure you've selected your country in the settings. If specific holidays are missing, you can add custom holidays.
