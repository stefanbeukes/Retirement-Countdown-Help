Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Retirement Countdown ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When using our App, you may provide the following information:

  • Your retirement date
  • Working days preferences
  • Vacation day information
  • Country location (for holiday calculations)
  • Custom holidays
  • Widget display preferences

Automatically Collected Information

We do not automatically collect any personal information. All data is stored locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Calculate your retirement countdown
  • Display relevant information in the app and widget
  • Provide country-specific holiday information

All calculations and data processing occur locally on your device.

Data Storage and Security

All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage mechanisms. We do not transmit or store your data on external servers.

Third-Party Services

Our App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. Your information never leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
